Privacy Policy for Save Up

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

Effective Date: December 26, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Save Up ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how Save Up collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using Save Up, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Display name and profile picture (from Google account)

Financial Data:

  • Transaction records (amount, category, date, description, notes)
  • Budget information (monthly budgets, spending limits)
  • Savings goals (goal names, target amounts, deadlines)
  • Fixed expenses (recurring bills, rent, subscriptions)
  • Loan information (loan amounts, interest rates, payment schedules)
  • Net worth tracking data (assets, liabilities)
  • Income records (salary, payday settings)

User Preferences:

  • Currency selection
  • Calendar system preference (Nepali BS or English AD)
  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Daily reminder time settings
  • Biometric authentication preferences
  • AI configuration (Gemini API key, if provided)

User-Generated Content:

  • Shopping lists
  • Todo lists
  • Countdown events
  • Notes
  • Receipt images (for AI scanning)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Information:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version (Android)
  • App version
  • Device identifiers (for crash reporting)

Usage Data:

  • App usage patterns
  • Feature interactions
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Performance metrics

Location Data:

  • We do NOT collect precise location data
  • Timezone information is used for notification scheduling

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Account Management: Authenticate users and maintain account security
  • Data Synchronization: Sync your financial data across devices via Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • Expense Tracking: Store and organize your transactions, budgets, and goals
  • Notifications: Send daily reminders at your preferred time
  • AI Insights: Generate financial insights and analyze receipts using Google Gemini AI (only if you provide an API key)

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Crash Reporting: Identify and fix bugs using Firebase Crashlytics
  • Performance Monitoring: Optimize app performance and user experience
  • Feature Development: Understand which features are most valuable to users

2.3 Security

  • Fraud Prevention: Detect and prevent unauthorized access
  • Data Protection: Implement security measures to protect your information
  • Beta Access Control: Manage access through redeem code system

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where We Store Data

  • Cloud Storage: Your data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Local Storage: Data is cached locally on your device using SQLite database
  • Server Location: Data is stored in Google Cloud servers (region: as per Firebase default)

4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Authentication: Secure authentication via Google Sign-In
  • Biometric Protection: Optional biometric authentication (fingerprint/face unlock)
  • Access Control: Firebase security rules restrict data access to authenticated users only
  • Code Obfuscation: App code is obfuscated to prevent reverse engineering

4.3 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Backups: Backup data is retained for 30 days for recovery purposes

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services (Firebase, Google) to operate our app
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (users will be notified)
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Save Up, our users, or the public

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: View all data we have collected about you
  • Export Your Data: Download your data in PDF or CSV format
  • Modify Your Data: Edit or update your information at any time
  • Delete Your Data: Request deletion of your account and all associated data

6.2 How to Exercise Your Rights
